Scenic Drive Experiences

Irish Countryside Drive (Sep 20)

Enjoy a picturesque drive through the lush Irish countryside on your way to the Irish National Stud. The route takes you west from Dublin along the R148/N4/M4 toward Kildare (75 km, approx. 1 hour). After exploring the Stud Farm, continue on a beautiful countryside loop:

  • Kilkea Castle: One of Ireland's oldest inhabited castles dating from 1180, offering perfect photo opportunities of medieval architecture
  • Burtown House & Gardens: Georgian villa surrounded by spectacular gardens and woodlands
  • Optional: Russborough House - magnificent Palladian mansion with extensive art collection

Total driving loop: 130 km with stunning rural landscapes throughout

Howth Peninsula Coastal Drive (Sep 21)

A scenic 30-minute drive (15 km) along Dublin Bay via the R105/Clontarf Road, offering beautiful coastal views. The route hugs the coastline, providing continuous sea vistas as you approach the charming fishing village of Howth. After your cliff walk and boat tour, explore further:

  • Howth Village: Weekend craft and food market, historic harbor
  • Optional: Continue to either Bull Island Nature Reserve (UNESCO Biosphere) or Malahide Castle (12th-century castle with gardens)

Round trip: 30-40 km of breathtaking coastal scenery

Important Travel Authorization Information

UK ETA (Electronic Travel Authorization)

REQUIRED FOR THIS TRIP - Starting January 8, 2025

US citizens traveling to the United Kingdom (including Northern Ireland) need to obtain an Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) if they do not have a UK visa or legal residency in the UK or the Republic of Ireland.

What You Need to Know:

  • Application: Online through the UK ETA app or official GOV.UK website
  • Cost: £10 (approximately $13) currently, increasing to £16 (approximately $20) from April 9, 2025
  • Processing time: Most decisions are immediate, but allow up to three working days
  • Validity: 2 years or until your passport expires, whichever comes first
  • Multiple entries: Valid for multiple visits to the UK

YOU WILL NEED TO APPLY FOR THIS BEFORE YOUR TRIP as your entry to London will occur after January 8, 2025.

For more information: UK ETA Official Website
