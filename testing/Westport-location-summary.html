📍 Westport Family Guide

🗺️ Overview

Westport is a charming heritage town in County Mayo, known for its Georgian architecture and family-friendly atmosphere. The town center is easily walkable with a stroller, featuring wide pavements and multiple rest areas.

Baby Care Essentials:
  • Baby supplies available at McCormack's Pharmacy (North Mall)
  • 24-hour emergency care at Mayo University Hospital (30 min drive)
  • Multiple parent-baby rooms throughout town

👶 Baby-Friendly Attractions

Westport House & Gardens

  • Opening hours: 10:00-16:00 (March-October)
  • Stroller-friendly paths
  • Clean changing facilities
  • Quiet areas for feeding
  • Admission: €14 adults, under 2 free

The Great Western Greenway

  • Accessible sections near town
  • Smooth surface for strollers
  • Multiple rest points
  • Free admission

Westport Town Park

  • Shaded seating areas
  • Baby-safe play areas
  • Clean facilities
  • Free admission
