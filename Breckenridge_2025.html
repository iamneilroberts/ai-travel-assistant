December 18-23, 2025
Your Customized Family Adventure
Breckenridge Trip Proposal

Hi, Kyle. This proposal includes slopeside lodging at Beaver Run Resort and Epic 3 Day Restricted Passes. I've planned activities for everyone: while you, Tanner, and Carter enjoy the slopes, Jennifer can explore the historic district, enjoy wine experiences, and relax at the resort's spa. The itinerary features family activities like the Monster Truck Sleigh Ride and snowmobiling to the Continental Divide.

I recommend booking airfare and lodging ASAP as prices will only increase. We can finalize other details like equipment rentals and additional activities later.

Trip Overview

Dates: December 18-23, 2025 (5 nights)

Travelers: Kyle, Jennifer, Tanner (20), and Carter (14)

Accommodation: Beaver Run Resort (2-bedroom slopeside condo)

Mountain Travel Essentials
  • Resort Elevation: 9,600 feet (stay hydrated and acclimate slowly)
  • Weather: December temperatures typically range from 14°F to 32°F with frequent snowfall
  • Transportation: The free Summit Stage bus provides convenient service throughout town and don't forget your ID if you're going to the dispensary!
  • Rentals: Pre-book equipment at least 2 weeks before arrival
  • Dining: Make dinner reservations in advance, especially for weekend nights

Accommodations

Beaver Run Resort
Slopeside 2-bedroom condo

Address: 620 Village Rd, Breckenridge, Colorado

Price: $3,140.00 (+$667.47 taxes)

Location: Ski-in/ski-out property located on the ski run above the base of Peak 9

Unit Features: 1,125 sq ft condo with 1 queen bed per bedroom, full kitchen, wood-burning fireplace, private balcony, and queen sleeper sofa

Resort Amenities:

  • Indoor/outdoor heated pool and second outdoor pool
  • Seven outdoor hot tubs
  • Fitness center
  • Multiple bars and restaurants
  • Ski and snowboard rental shops on-site
  • Retail stores and mini market/deli
  • Video arcade and game room
  • Complimentary in-town shuttle
  • Complimentary wireless Internet
  • Guest laundry facilities

Why We Chose This Property: Beaver Run Resort is one of Breckenridge's most popular properties, offering world-class amenities and convenient access to the slopes. Perfect for families seeking both comfort and ski convenience.

Skiing Details

Epic 3 Day Restricted Pass

For: Kyle, Tanner, and Carter

Cost: $335.00 per person ($1,005 total)

Valid: December 19-22, 2025

Not valid: Nov 28-29, Dec 26-31, 2025, Jan 17 and Feb 14-15, 2026

Pickup: Breckenridge Resort Ticket Office (Dec 18)

Description: Experience three full days of skiing at Breckenridge's legendary terrain spanning five peaks and nearly 3,000 acres. Your Epic 3 Day Restricted Pass provides access to some of the best skiing in Colorado, with terrain suitable for all levels from beginner to expert.

Dining Recommendations

Kim's Breakfast Tips

Columbine Cafe (109 S Main St) opens early and has great breakfast burritos and cinnamon rolls.

Cabin Coffee Co. (222 S Main St) is perfect for grab-and-go breakfast sandwiches and coffee before hitting the slopes.

Daybreak - A Breakfast Place (305 N Main St) - This local favorite opens at 7am. Their mountaineer breakfast skillet is perfect fuel for a ski day. Arrive before 8am to beat the crowds!
