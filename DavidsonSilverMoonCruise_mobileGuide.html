Kim's Inside Passage Viewing Guide:

For the best wildlife viewing and scenery:

  • Morning (6:00-10:00 AM): Pool deck (Deck 10)
    - Coffee service available
    - Best early morning whale sightings
    - Complimentary binoculars at reception
  • Afternoon (2:00-6:00 PM): Your veranda or Panorama Lounge
    - Peak time for wildlife
    - Tea service in Panorama Lounge
  • What to Watch For:
    - Orcas (especially in the morning)
    - Humpback whales (afternoon is best)
    - Bald eagles near the shoreline
    - Dolphins (often follow the ship)
