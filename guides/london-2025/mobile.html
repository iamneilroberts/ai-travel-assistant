Tower of London

St Katharine's & Wapping, London EC3N 4AB

Open in Maps

10:30 AM - 1:00 PM

Key Features:

  • Crown Jewels (go here first to avoid lines)
  • White Tower - Medieval Palace
  • Yeoman Warder tour (free, 60 mins)
  • Medieval Chapel Royal of St Peter ad Vincula

Tips:

  • Book tickets online in advance (£29.90 per person)
  • Join first Yeoman Warder tour at 10:45 AM
  • Cafe on premises for lunch
  • Gift shop near exit

City Cruises - Tower Pier

1:30 PM - 3:00 PM

Route Details:

  • Tower Pier to Westminster Pier
  • Pass under Tower Bridge
  • Views of Shakespeare's Globe
  • Houses of Parliament
  • London Eye

Tips:

  • Book one-way ticket (£14.50 per person)
  • Covered and open-air seating available
  • Snacks and drinks available onboard
  • Best photos from open-air upper deck

British Museum

Great Russell St, Bloomsbury, London WC1B 3DG

Open in Maps

3:30 PM - 5:00 PM

90-Minute Highlights:

  • Rosetta Stone (Room 4)
  • Egyptian Mummies (Room 62-63)
  • Parthenon Sculptures (Room 18)
  • Lewis Chessmen (Room 40)
  • Assyrian Lion Hunt (Room 10)

Tips:

  • Free admission
  • Use Great Court entrance (shortest queues)
  • Free map available at information desk
  • Cafe and gift shop in Great Court
  • Bag check required for large bags
