Bath Day Trip Details

Essential Information

Duration: Full day (8-10 hours)
Distance from London: 115 miles (185 km)
Travel Time: 1.5 hours by train

Pro Tip: Book train tickets in advance for the best prices. Trains depart from London Paddington station.

6:00 PM
